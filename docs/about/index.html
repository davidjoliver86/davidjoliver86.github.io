About

2020-11-29 — 2 min read

I’m David - or “Davido” as certain folks may know me as. I find “Davido” more fun to say. I’m a Senior Associate Cloud Engineer at ISS. I love working with AWS, Python, and infrastructure as code. When I’m not developing cloud solutions, I’m dropping my initials on various pinball machines.

In 2009, I started my career as a data analyst at an up-and-coming startup called BrightScope knowing very little about coding. What started as an intuition on how to query and change our datasets led me down a path of self-taught SQL and Python. The engineers at the time realized that software engineering is a teachable skill. Using myself as the proto-example, the “Pod” - as they named it - has “graduated” over 10 successful “analyst turned engineers”.

Over the past 10 years, spanning two acquisitions, I’ve grown from a data analyst to a Python/Django developer to an AWS cloud engineer. This is an exciting time to be in DevOps and I look forward to continually expanding my skill set.

I’m also ranked among the top 250 pinball players in the world, out of over 60,000. So I’m basically a 1%-er. If you’ve ever played in a tournament, you’re ranked.
